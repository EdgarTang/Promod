Promod's Innovative Solutions

Promod panels are water resistant and have been tested to Australian Standard 4284:2008 Weatherproofing - PASSED

Non-combustible - Tested to Australian Standard 1530.1 Non-combustibility - PASSED

Tested to Australian Standard 1530.4. Promod panels achieved a Fire Resistance Level (FRL) of 60/60/60 & 90/90/90

Acoustic qualities - Excellent! Promod's Fireless Party Wall System achieves an acoustic rating of no less than Rw+Ctr 50

Property values - Can be finished with any external, ascetically pleasing finishing system that enhances the property's value.

Maintenance - Low maintenance due to the panels rot resistance and longevity

Durability - High Impact.

Mould/mildew - Promod panels do not support the growth of mildew or mold.

Thermal qualities - Promod panels have excellent thermal qualities.

Building costs - Only standard carpentry tools are required to install Promod panels and combined with quick installation systems.

Environmentally friendly - Many environmental benefits come with Promod panels, one being the mining of the main element, magnesium.

The curing process captures carbon dioxide and is conducted at room temperature which makes the curing of our Promod panels an extremely green process.
